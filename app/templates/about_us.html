Our Mission

At Neumark, we believe that early awareness saves lives. Our mission is to provide accessible, data-driven risk assessments that empower individuals to take proactive steps toward their respiratory health before symptoms appear.

By combining multiple machine learning models with comprehensive health data, we aim to reduce the global burden of lung cancer through early detection and prevention.

Our Methodology

1

Comprehensive Data Collection

Our assessment captures 23 key factors including age, gender, environmental exposures (air pollution, occupational hazards), lifestyle factors (smoking, alcohol use, diet), medical history (genetic risk, chronic lung disease), and symptoms (chest pain, coughing of blood, fatigue, shortness of breath).

2

Advanced Multi-Model Analysis

Your data is processed through three distinct machine learning models (Random Forest, XGBoost, and Logistic Regression) with appropriate feature normalization. We then calculate a weighted ensemble prediction that leverages the strengths of each algorithm, producing a more reliable risk assessment than any single model could provide.

3

Risk Factor Categorization

We analyze your individual risk factors using specialized thresholds to identify both positive health factors and areas of concern. For each risk factor, we determine whether high or low values represent increased risk, allowing for personalized recommendations based on your specific profile.

4

Personalized Report with Actionable Recommendations

You receive a comprehensive risk assessment with your overall risk level, detailed model scores, and personalized recommendations prioritized by your highest risk factors. For high-risk individuals, we recommend clinical screening, while providing tailored lifestyle modifications for all risk levels.

Medical Disclaimer

The Neumark assessment tool provides an estimation of risk based on known factors and is not a diagnostic tool. Results should be discussed with healthcare professionals and are not a substitute for medical screening or consultation.

If you are experiencing symptoms such as chest pain, coughing of blood, or unexplained weight loss, please consult with a healthcare provider immediately. Early medical attention is crucial for respiratory conditions.
